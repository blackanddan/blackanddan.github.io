Personas

Predominantly focusing on those who remain in areas of the world of knowledge for local weather. The website is aimed towards those who are seeking daily information on the weather. Typically those are considered the average consumer that are seeking a simple, basic outline of the day.
The other area we're focusing on are those who are more of the outdoors group of people. Hiking, biking, hammocking, you name it. We're aiming to make the website easily accessable.

Scenario's

1. Those who wake up in the morning and are deciding what to weather for the day.
2. Preparing for a hike in during the day and want to know the outlook of the weather.
3. Traveling abroad to another state.
4. Camping for the weekend with family or friends.
5. Checking out the next weeks forcast for future plans.
